Recruiter

Simultaneously serving as the Assistant Manager in two different offices (St. Paul and Columbus), I performed well and was promoted to Branch Manager in 2017.

Front-End Web Developer

As a self-taught web designer and developer, I possess a forte for problem-solving, solutin-driven research, and collaboration. I began my web designing journey with studying basic HTML and CSS concepts; eventually, I expanded upon my capabilites and built simple websites that conveyed what the client or I wanted.

Sales Representative

Through Vector, I have four years of direct sales experience via Cutco products. I have conducted over 60+ consultation appointments -- both in-home and virtually -- with customers. During these appointments, I prioritized actively-listening to customers, promptly providing information of value, and genuinely seeking the best option/deal to ensure the families satisfaction with their products. Due to my high regard for quality customer service and professional authenticity when working with customers, I am personally responsible for the generation of $150,000 in sales results and held many records for in my local office.

Entrepreneur

During my tenure at Vector, I independently ran both Branch offices and a District office. While serving as a young entrepreneur and leader, I interviewed over 1400 applicants and personally recruited 600+ applicants via college campuses and social media sites, such as Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat. Furthermore, on multiple occasions I built teams of 75+ individuals -- from scratch -- within 4 months, both generated $82,000 in sales and produced a profit in a single campaign, all position during my spring and fall semesters.

Ispired and grateful for leadership opportunities Vector afforded me, I founded InSil3nc3 as a continuation of the skills and opportunity gained during my time with Vector. Founded in September 2019, InSil3nc3, LLC is a silent disco, movie and event promotion and entertainment company. InSil3nc3 is based out of my hometown -- Columbus, GA -- and was created with the intent to provide an alternative form of entertainment to persons in the southeastern US.
